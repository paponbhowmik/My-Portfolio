About Me

My journey in tech started in 2020, not in a classroom, but from simple curiosity. I was just searching online, wondering how websites are made. That small interest slowly grew into something bigger — I started learning HTML and CSS on my own. In 2021, I joined a front-end development course. There, I learned how to build websites using HTML, CSS, Bootstrap, jQuery, and some JavaScript. I also discovered something new — UI/UX design with Figma. That’s when I realized I don’t just enjoy coding; I also love creating beautiful and user-friendly designs. Even though I was learning and practicing, finding work wasn’t easy. But I didn’t stop. In 2022, I started learning Python and Django because I wanted to build complete websites — both front-end and back-end. Now, I’m studying at university in the MCT (Multimedia and Creative Technologies) department. I’m expected to graduate in 2028. Along the way, I also learned Photoshop and Illustrator to improve my design skills. I believe learning never stops. Every project I work on teaches me something new. I love turning ideas into real, working websites — mixing both design and code to build things that are useful and meaningful. This is more than a career for me — it’s a journey I truly enjoy.
